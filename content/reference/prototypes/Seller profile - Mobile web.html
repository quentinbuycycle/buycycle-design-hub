TRI-3636 — Star rating → anchor link
Tapping the star rating row in the profile header scrolls to this Ratings tab. The "(23) →" arrow is the visual affordance.
📐
TRI-3636 — Auto-feedback hidden
buycycle auto-generates a 5★ for completed transactions where the buyer left no review. These entries are NOT shown as review cards. They ARE counted in the total (23 includes auto-entries). Showing them would feel fabricated.

💬 @jonasjaeger — Suggestion: consider applying this hiding logic only in the edge case where a seller has exactly 1 review and it is auto-generated. In that scenario it dominates the display and is misleading. For sellers with 2+ real reviews, the auto-entry is already diluted — showing it may be acceptable and reduces engineering scope.
Display = real reviews only  ·  Count = real + auto
